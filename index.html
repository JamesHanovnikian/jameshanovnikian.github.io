Capstone

For my capstone project, I decided to make an app that makes skate spots easier to find for rollerbladers. I grew up as an avid aggressive rollerblader and I often struggled to find good skate spots to my liking. In my opinion, an important aspect of the sport is the community aspect; Many times if you do not know the best places and people to skate with, you will not progress as much in the sport. The perfect cocktail of creativity, inspiration, and motivation from others is necessary for you to try tricks that put you outside of your comfort zone.


BladeSpots: I set out to make an app where people can easily find the best skate spots in their area and filter based on category and distance. The skate spots are user generated and include relevant information about each spot. On the page of each spot, users can also share which tricks they have completed there to offer inspiration for others to try similar tricks. BladeSpots was built using Ruby on Rails and Vue.js, utilizing Mapbox API to display pins on an interactive map. I implemented Bootstraps for styling.
