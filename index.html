Capstone

When tasked with making a capstone project for my coding bootcamp, I decided to make an app that makes skate spots easier to find for rollerbladers. I grew up as an avid aggressive rollerblader and I often struggled to find good skate spots. In my opinion, a vital part of the sport is the community aspect; Many times if you don't know the best places and people to skate with, you won't progress in the sport. The cocktail of creativity, inspiration, and motivation of others is necessary to push you forward to put your body on the line to try a new trick.


BladeSpots: I set out to make an app where people can easily find the best skate spots in their area and filter based on category and distance. The skate spots are user generated and include information about each spot. The app also features a place to share what tricks you have completed at each spot to compare with others and get inspiration on tricks to try next. BladeSpots was built using Ruby on Rails and Vue.js, utilizing Mapbox API to display pins on an interactive map.
